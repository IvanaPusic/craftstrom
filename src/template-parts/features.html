Plug&Play

Our solar sets and batteries are fully plug&play, meaning you don't need anything but an outlet.

Smart Technology

We were the first to fully integrate our solar and battery system with a powermeter. This way, you can accurately control every aspect in Milliseconds.

Setup at Home

Our solar and battery system are not connected directly. Instead, they use your home power grid to exchange power when needed. You can place your battery wherever you like it best.

Uninterruptible Power Supply

Place our battery anywhere in your home for convenient access during power outages. Plug your fridge or medical devices into the off-grid inverter on top, and keep the battery connected to an outlet. This ensures your appliances automatically draw power from the battery when the power goes out.
