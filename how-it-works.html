Install Power Meter

Power meter measures appliance power usage, enabling permit-free solar system operation by balancing solar production with consumption.

It monitors home usage, allowing solar panels to reduce purchased electricity accordingly.

Excess solar production is managed by adjusting inverter output or instructing the Smart Battery to store surplus energy.

Store your power for when you need it most.

Your battery plugs into a standard wall outlet anywhere in your home and communicates with your solar system. They’ll automatically become best friends and start cooperating.

In the event of a power outage or you want to run products on the go, simply pull the battery out of the smart base and place it where you need it most.

Types of our solar system

ON GRID- DAY TIME

On grid solar system during the day is connected to the power grid. The energy produced by the solar panels can be used immediately in the household, and the excess energy is stored in batteries.

ON GRID- NIGHT TIME

On grid solar system during the night is not connected to the power grid. The energy produced by the solar panels, which was stored in batteries during the day powers your home through the batteries at night.

OFF GRID

An off-grid solar system is a stand-alone power system that operates independently from the public electricity grid.
