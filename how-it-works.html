Install Power Meter

Our Power Meter measures the appliances power usage, enabling permit-free solar system operation by balancing solar production with consumption. It monitors your homes usage in real time, allowing the solar panels to drastically reduce the amount of purchased electricity. Excess solar production is throttled on the inverter side or stored in the Smart Battery for night usage or outages.

How much can I power with the kits

ON GRID - DAY TIME

The on grid solar system is connected to the power grid. The energy produced by the Smart Solar Kits can power your home immediately, and the excess energy is stored in batteries for at night. Both the Smart Solar Kit and the Smart Batteries can team up to lower your electric bill during the day!

ON GRID - NIGHT TIME

The excess energy produced by the solar panels during the day was stored in the Smart Batteries and can now power your home at night.

OFF GRID

An off-grid solar system is a stand-alone power unit that operates independently from the public electricity grid. It can power some appliances during an outage or power your outdoor adventures!
