How it works?

01 Plug your panels into an outdoor power outlet.

02 Register all devices with our app to monitor and control. PRODUCTION BEGINS IMMEDIATELY AFTER PLUGGING THE PANELS INTO THE OUTLET!

Our Smart Solar System will replace electricity that you usually buy from your local utility. The solar energy you have converted will automatically power your appliances. Should you produce more electricity than the house needs, the Smart Battery will act as an appliance and start charging!  You will use the stored energy to feed power back to your appliances once the sun goes down.

Off Grid Inverter

The Off-Grid Inverter is placed on top of the battery.

It holds two AC outlets (2x600W nominal – 1200W surge) and a high-speed wireless charger. It comes with the purchase of the main smart battery. Is one enough? If you want to power devices in different rooms during the night or during a power outage, then this is more convenient than using long extension cords. Also, the battery will last longer with only one device connected to it…

Smart Battery

Smart battery stores energy generated from solar panels for later use. It can be lifted out of the base to power appliances during an outage or on your outdoor adventures. BMS (Battery Management System), DC port & charge controller for off grid use is incorporated in each Battery. You can charge Battery directly from our 3x200W ultra-thin & flexible Bi-facial Panels. Charge time about 2h! It has a display that shows you the % of charge level in real time. Below the display are 2x USB Type A, 1x USB Type C. The Battery has up to 8500 Charge cycles.

About

Measure your appliances’ power usage with Power Meter. Keeps your solar system permit-free by balancing solar production and appliances’ power consumption. Our Power Meter Measures the Usage of the home and allows the panels to lower that amount of bought electricity. In the case the home needs less power than the Solar kit can produce we simply throttle the output of the inverters or tell the Smart Battery to initiate a charge to store power for later. This refined balancing act of communication is ensured at all times! All of our products use our patented communication method through wifi & mHz Communication in parallel. Our products communicate with each other even if the wifi is down or spotty.

About

In a solar power system, an inverter is a crucial component that converts the direct current (DC) electricity generated by solar panels into alternating current (AC) electricity. AC e is the standard form of electrical power used by homes and businesses, and it is what most household appliances and electronics require to operate. In the case that the home needs than the Solar kit can produce, we simply throttle the output of the inverters! Our Smart Inverter also uses our patented communication method through WIFI & mHz Communication in Our Inverters communicate with the Power Meter even if the WIFI is down or spotty.

How it works

01 Solar panels capture sunlight and convert it into direct current (DC) electricity, which flows through connected wires.
02 The DC electricity from the solar panels is fed into the inverter, which converts it into alternating current (AC) electricity for use in homes and businesses.
02 The converted alternating current then distributed to power various appliances and battery for later use.
