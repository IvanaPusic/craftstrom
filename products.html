How it works?

01 Connect your solar panels to a Smart battery and store energy generated from solar panels for later use. All you need is outlet!

02 During the night, when solar panels are not producing electricity your home draws power from the batteries!

03 You can use the Battery to power you on the go as well. Simply take it anywhere you need power, camping for example!

The batteries operate within a UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) system while remaining connected to the utility grid. This setup enables you to connect your refrigerator directly to the batteries. Therefore, if there is a power outage, the refrigerator continues to function using power from the batteries, while still being part of your home network grid. This arrangement ensures that critical appliances remain operational even during electricity disruptions, without disconnecting them from the overall home power system.

About

Measure your appliances’ power usage with Power Meter. Keeps your solar system permit-free by balancing solar production and appliances’ power consumption. Our Power Meter Measures the Usage of the home and allows the panels to lower that amount of bought electricity. In the case the home needs less power than the Solar kit can produce we simply throttle the output of the inverters or tell the Smart Battery to initiate a charge to store power for later. This refined balancing act of communication is ensured at all times! All of our products use our patented communication method through wifi & mHz Communication in parallel. Our products communicate with each other even if the wifi is down or spotty.

About

In a solar power system, an inverter is a crucial component that converts the direct current (DC) electricity generated by solar panels into alternating current (AC) electricity. AC electricity is the standard form of electrical power used by homes and businesses, and it is what most household appliances and electronics require to operate. In the case that the home needs less power than the Solar kit can produce, we simply throttle the output of the inverters! Our Smart Inverter also uses our patented communication method through WIFI & mHz Communication in parallel. Our Inverters communicate with the Power Meter even if the WIFI is down or spotty.

How it works?

01 Solar panels capture sunlight and convert it into direct current (DC) electricity, which flows through connected wires.

02 The DC electricity from the solar panels is fed into the inverter, which converts it into alternating current (AC) electricity for use in homes and businesses.

03 The converted alternating current then distributed to power various appliances and battery for later use.

The batteries operate within a UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) system while remaining connected to the utility grid. This setup enables you to connect your refrigerator directly to the batteries. Therefore, if there is a power outage, the refrigerator continues to function using power from the batteries, while still being part of your home network grid. This arrangement ensures that critical appliances remain operational even during electricity disruptions, without disconnecting them from the overall home power system.
