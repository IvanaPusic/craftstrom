Our products

Meet The Solar Panels

Ultra thin Panels with Smart Inverter. Lightweight (3kg), Semi-flexible and Bi-facial. Completely Modular, in steps of 400Watt (400W - 800W - 1200W -1600W - 2000W). Solar production will reduce if excess generation exceeds household usage to prevent feeding electricity back to the utility grid.

Meet the Battery

On-grid and off-grid capabilities. Store your power for when you need it most. Plug into any standard wall outlet in your home and it communicates with your solar system. During a power outage or for portable use, easily detach the battery from the smart base and place it where needed.

Meet the Power meter

Measures home usage, enabling solar panels to offset purchased electricity. Ensures optimal energy balancing between solar and battery through communication. Utilizes Wi-Fi and MHz communication for reliable connectivity. Maintains communication during Wi-Fi interruptions or low signal.

Plug&Play

Our solar sets and batteries are fully plug&play, meaning you don't need anything but an outlet.

Smart Technology

We were the first to fully integrate our solar and battery system with a powermeter. This way, you can accurately control every aspect in milliseconds.

Setup at Home

Our solar and battery system are not connected directly. Instead, they use your home power grid to exchange power when needed. You can place your battery wherever you like it best.

Uninterruptible Power Supply

Place our battery anywhere in your home for convenient access during power outages. Plug your fridge or medical devices into the off-grid inverter on top, and keep the battery connected to an outlet. This ensures your appliances automatically draw power from the battery when the power goes out.

System reaction time of <1 second for accuracy

Unlike competitors in the market, our products use our proprietary communication system to talk to each other, sending data every 0.1 seconds. This ensures that you won’t accidentally use or transmit power when you don’t want to.

No Interconnection Agreement via Zero-Export Functionality

Our products communicate every 0.1 seconds, eliminating the need for an interconnection agreement by avoiding power export. Unlike slow WiFi, our direct communication ensures instant control.

National Electric Code Safe

We have designed the Safety Gate Adapter, which is an intelligent breaker. Use this, if you don’t have a dedicated outlet (leading directly to the breaker box without any other outlets). It constantly monitors that line and turns off our products, if you need more power than is advisable in connection with solar input.

Extreme Flexibility in Your Use via the App

Our app offers flexible control over your charging cycles and power. To save on your power bill, use our auto algorithm to optimise charging and discharging. For quick readiness, charge your battery immediately. For arbitrage savings, schedule different charging times each day using the calendar function.

Open API

Since we’re all geeks here, we have, of course, opened up our software to you, the user. This allows you to pull your own data and connect with your devices.

Communication Safety

CraftStrom products do not require your home WiFi to balance your energy household. Once you setup all your devices at home, they will create their own communication network that can’t be hacked through your home internet.

Types of our solar system

ON GRID- DAY TIME

On grid solar system during the day is connected to the power grid. The energy produced by the solar panels can be used immediately in the household, and the excess energy is stored in batteries.

ON GRID- NIGHT TIME

On grid solar system during the night is not connected to the power grid. The energy produced by the solar panels, which was stored in batteries during the day powers your home through the batteries at night.

OFF GRID

An off-grid solar system is a stand-alone power system that operates independently from the public electricity grid.
