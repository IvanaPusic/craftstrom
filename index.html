Uninterrupted Power Supply

Place our battery anywhere in your home for convenient access during power outages. Plug your fridge or medical devices into the off-grid inverter on top, and keep the battery connected to an outlet. This ensures your appliances automatically draw power from the battery when the power goes out.

Extreme Flexibility in Your Use via the App

Our app offers flexible control over your charging cycles and power. To save on your power bill, use our auto algorithm to optimise charging and discharging. For quick readiness, charge your battery immediately. For arbitrage savings, schedule different charging times each day using the calendar function.

No Adventures without Power

Since we live in a time where everything is powered by electricity, we made sure that you can use our products completely offgrid. When you’re not at home, simply take our battery out of its smart base and bring the solar panels with you. You can plug 3 panels directly in our battery and charge it full in 2 hours.

No Energy Losses For Heating or Cooling

Since your can place your battery anywhere in your home, it doesn’t require internal heating or cooling systems that draw massive power away from where you need it most – at home!

How much can I power with the kits

ON GRID - DAY TIME

The on grid solar system is connected to the power grid. The energy produced by the Smart Solar Kits can power your home immediately, and the excess energy is stored in batteries for at night. Both the Smart Solar Kit and the Smart Batteries can team up to lower your electric bill during the day!

ON GRID - NIGHT TIME

The excess energy produced by the solar panels during the day was stored in the Smart Batteries and can now power your home at night.

OFF GRID

An off-grid solar system is a stand-alone power unit that operates independently from the public electricity grid. It can power some appliances during an outage or power your outdoor adventures!
