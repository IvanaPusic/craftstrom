1kWh Smart Battery

Connect your solar panels to a Smart battery and store energy generated from solar panels for later use. All you need is outlet!

1kWh Extension Battery

This is the same battery as the “Smart battery”, but it is secondary which means that it is bought after you already own one battery and therefore it does not come with a smart base but connects to a smart base battery that you have already bought. You can add up to 3 Batteries to Smart Base.

In a solar power system, an inverter is a crucial component that converts the direct current (DC) electricity generated by solar panels into alternating current (AC) electricity. Our Inverters communicate with the Power Meter even if the WIFI is down or spotty.

We have designed the Safety Gate Adapter, which is an intelligent breaker. Use this, if you don’t have a dedicated outlet (leading directly to the breaker box without any other outlets). It constantly monitors that line and turns off our products, if you need more power than is advisable in connection with solar input.
